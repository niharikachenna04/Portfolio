Course Overview

This course explores how Artificial Intelligence is applied in architecture, engineering, and construction, including current use cases, future impact, and practical applications in project workflows.

Course title

CE 895: AI in the AEC Industry

This course focuses on how AI is currently being used in the AEC industry, where it is headed, and how it impacts different construction roles and workflows.

Course description

What the course covers

The course combines conceptual understanding with practical exploration of AI tools applied to real-world construction tasks.

It introduces the growing role of AI in planning, documentation, estimating, analysis, and project decision-making.

Course objectives

Main learning goals

  • Understand the role of AI in the AEC industry
  • Analyze how AI impacts traditional construction workflows
  • Apply AI tools to tasks such as documentation, estimating, and analysis
  • Explore task-specific AI tools used in construction
  • Recognize ethical and practical considerations of AI adoption
Key themes

Topics explored in the course

The course highlights how AI supports efficiency, productivity, and decision-making across the built environment.

It also examines both the opportunities and limitations of AI use in construction practice.

Course value

Why this course matters

This course helped build a stronger understanding of how emerging technologies can connect with real construction workflows and industry needs.
