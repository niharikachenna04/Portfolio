Course Description

AI in Construction and Facilities Management

This course examined how artificial intelligence can influence the built environment through smarter analysis, automation, communication, and decision support. It created space to explore both the opportunities and the limitations of AI across project planning, construction workflows, operations, and facilities-related systems.

Why It Matters
  • AI is changing how information is generated, organized, and communicated.
  • Construction and facilities work involve large amounts of fragmented data.
  • Digital tools can support efficiency, analysis, reporting, and workflow improvement.
  • Students need both technical curiosity and critical thinking to assess AI use cases.
