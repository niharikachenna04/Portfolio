About Me

I am a construction management graduate student with an architectural background and a growing interest in how AI can support project delivery, decision-making, facility operations, and communication across the built environment.

Academic background

From architecture to construction management

My academic journey began in architecture, where I developed a strong understanding of design, drawings, and spatial planning.

Over time, I became more interested in how projects are executed on site, which led me to pursue construction management.

This transition helped me focus more on coordination, documentation, and project delivery.

Working with AI tools

Using AI in a practical way

Through this course, I explored different AI tools and how they can support tasks such as documentation, summarization, and estimating.

Rather than replacing existing workflows, these tools can assist in improving efficiency and reducing repetitive tasks when used appropriately.

Perspective

Connecting design and execution

My architectural background helps me understand design intent, while my construction training helps me focus on how that design is executed in practice.

I am able to connect drawings with real-world construction processes, improve coordination between teams, and reduce potential issues during execution.
