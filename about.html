My Story

From architecture to AI-aware construction thinking.

My academic and professional journey began in architecture, where I developed an eye for design, spatial thinking, and the importance of clarity in visual communication. Over time, I became more interested in what happens after the design phase—how teams coordinate, how information flows across a project, and how decisions affect execution in the real world.

That curiosity led me toward construction management and, more recently, toward the role of artificial intelligence in AEC and facilities work. This course gave me a chance to explore AI not only as a technology trend, but as a practical tool that can influence workflows, productivity, and problem-solving.
