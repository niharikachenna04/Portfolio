Project 02

Reviewing Custom GPTs

This project explores how custom-built GPT tools support both general and AEC-specific tasks. I tested two different GPTs to evaluate their usefulness, clarity, and real-world application.

Project Purpose

Why I reviewed these GPTs

The goal was to understand how Custom GPTs are designed for specific tasks and how users interact with them through real prompts.

This helped me evaluate how AI can act as a focused assistant for both general and technical workflows.

Featured GPT

Career Coach GPT

A general-purpose GPT focused on career guidance, resume building, and professional development.

AEC GPT

MS Project GPT

A task-specific GPT that helps explain scheduling concepts, dependencies, and project planning workflows.

Comparison

What I learned

Career Coach GPT

Best for general advice and career-related decisions.

MS Project GPT

Best for technical understanding and task-based workflows.

Main Difference

One is broad guidance, the other is focused and industry-specific.

Final Reflection

Custom GPTs work best when they are focused, structured, and connected to real tasks.
