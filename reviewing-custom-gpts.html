AI IN AEC INDUSTRY
Project 02

Reviewing Custom GPTs

This project focused on exploring how custom-built GPT tools can support users in different tasks. I reviewed one general-purpose GPT and one AEC-specific GPT to understand how they work, where they are useful, and what limitations they may have.

Project Purpose

Why I reviewed these GPTs

The goal of this assignment was to understand how Custom GPTs are designed for specific tasks and how users interact with them. Instead of only reading about AI tools, I tested them directly and evaluated how well they responded to real prompts.

This helped me understand how GPTs can act as focused assistants—some for general support like career planning, and others for technical workflows such as project scheduling in construction.

Featured GPT

Career Coach GPT

I used the Career Coach GPT to get advice about career planning, resume preparation, and job readiness. The responses were simple, clear, and helpful for someone preparing for future opportunities.

AEC GPT

MS Project GPT

I used the MS Project GPT to understand scheduling concepts such as tasks, durations, dependencies, and milestones. It helped make project planning easier to understand.

Comparison

What I learned from both tools

Career Coach GPT

Worked well for general career guidance, resumes, job preparation, and professional growth.

MS Project GPT

Worked well for explaining scheduling concepts and organizing project tasks clearly.

Main Difference

One provides general advice, while the other is focused on technical AEC workflows.

Strengths

What worked well

Both GPTs gave clear explanations, organized information well, and responded effectively to follow-up questions. They made learning faster and more structured.

Limitations

What could improve

Some responses were general. More personalization and step-by-step guidance would improve usability.

Final Reflection

Custom GPTs are most useful when they are focused, clear, and connected to real tasks.

They help users get quick guidance, organize information, and understand complex topics more easily.
