Project 02

Reviewing Custom GPTs

This project explores how custom-built GPT tools can support users in both general and AEC-specific tasks. I tested two different GPTs to understand how they perform, where they are useful, and where improvements are needed.

Project Purpose

Why I reviewed these GPTs

The goal of this assignment was to understand how Custom GPTs are designed for specific tasks and how users interact with them. Instead of only reading about AI tools, I tested them directly using real prompts.

This helped me understand how GPTs can act as focused assistants—some for general tasks like career advice, and others for technical workflows such as project scheduling.

Featured GPT

Career Coach GPT

This GPT helped with career planning, resume preparation, and skill development. It provided clear and simple advice that is useful for students and early professionals.

AEC GPT

MS Project GPT

This GPT explained scheduling concepts such as tasks, dependencies, and milestones, making project planning easier to understand for beginners in construction management.

Comparison

What I learned from both tools

Career Coach GPT

Strong in general advice, career planning, resumes, and professional growth.

MS Project GPT

Strong in explaining technical scheduling concepts and project workflows.

Main Difference

One focuses on general guidance, while the other is task-specific and industry-based.

Strengths

What worked well

Both GPTs provided clear explanations, organized information well, and responded effectively to follow-up questions.

Limitations

What could improve

Some responses were general. More personalization and step-by-step guidance would improve usability.

Final Reflection

Custom GPTs are most effective when they are focused, clear, and connected to a real task.
