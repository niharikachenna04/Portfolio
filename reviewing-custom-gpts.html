Project 02

Reviewing Custom GPTs

This project focused on exploring how custom-built mini-agents, such as Custom GPTs, can support users in different tasks. I reviewed one general-purpose Featured GPT and one AEC-related GPT to understand how these tools work, where they are useful, and what limitations they may have.

Project Purpose

Why I reviewed these GPTs

The goal of this assignment was to understand how Custom GPTs are designed for specific tasks and how users interact with them. Instead of only reading about AI tools, I used them directly and evaluated how well they responded to real prompts.

This helped me see how a GPT can act like a focused assistant. Some GPTs are useful for general support, such as career planning, while others can help with technical or industry tasks, such as project scheduling in construction.

Featured GPT

Career Coach GPT

I used the Career Coach GPT to get advice about career planning, resume preparation, skill improvement, and job readiness. The responses were clear, simple, and useful for someone who is preparing for future career opportunities.

AEC GPT

MS Project GPT

I used the MS Project GPT to understand project scheduling concepts. It explained tasks, durations, dependencies, milestones, and scheduling logic in a way that was easier to understand for a beginner.

Comparison

What I learned from both tools

Career Coach GPT

This GPT worked well for giving general career guidance. It helped me think about resumes, job preparation, skills, and professional growth.

MS Project GPT

This GPT worked well for explaining scheduling concepts. It made project planning easier to understand by organizing tasks and explaining milestones.

Main Difference

The Career Coach GPT was more general and advice-based, while the MS Project GPT was more task-focused and connected to construction project management.

What Worked Well

Strengths I noticed

The responses were clear and easy to understand. The GPTs helped organize information quickly, were useful for beginners learning a new topic, and responded well to follow-up questions. The MS Project GPT also made scheduling concepts easier to understand.

What Could Improve

Limitations I noticed

Some responses were general. The Career Coach GPT could ask more questions about the user's background before giving advice. The MS Project GPT could be stronger if it included more step-by-step instructions for using Microsoft Project software.

AEC Relevance

Why this matters for construction management

Custom GPTs can be valuable in the AEC industry because construction work involves many repeated tasks, technical explanations, planning steps, and communication needs. A focused GPT can help users understand scheduling, organize project information, prepare documents, and learn software workflows.

For students and entry-level professionals, this type of tool can make complex topics easier to approach. Instead of struggling with a blank page or confusing software concepts, users can ask questions and receive structured guidance.

Final Reflection

Reviewing these GPTs helped me understand that custom AI tools are most useful when they are focused, clear, and connected to a real task.

Overall, I think Custom GPTs are helpful because they give quick support in a specific area. They may not replace human judgment or detailed learning, but they can make the first step easier by explaining ideas, organizing information, and helping users move forward with more confidence.
